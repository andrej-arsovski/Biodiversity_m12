Belly Button Biodiversity Dashboard

Use the interactive charts below to explore the dataset

Our bodies contain an entire ecosystem of microorganisms. As we learn more about the organisms that inhabit our bodies, we find ways to benefit from them. In this work we examine and visualize the types of bacteria that colonize the belly buttons of different individuals. From the drop down menu the test subject can be seleced by their ID and in response the three graphs will change to display their unique bacterial biodiversity. More information about the individual is displayed in the "Demographic Info" table immediately below. The bar chart displays the ten most populous bacterial species found in their belly button. The gauge on the right shows how many times the individual washed their belly buttun per week. Finally, the bubble chart on the bottom displays all the bacterial species found and how many cultures of each were present in the subjects belly button.
